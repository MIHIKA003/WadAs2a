Welcome to PES Modern College of Engineering

Pes Modern College of Engineering, situated in Pune, is a beacon of educational excellence in the field of engineering. Established with a commitment to nurturing innovative thinkers and skilled professionals, the college boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure and a dynamic learning environment. With a rich history spanning decades, it has consistently upheld its reputation for academic rigor and industry relevance. The college offers a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines of engineering, catering to the diverse aspirations of its students. Emphasizing holistic development, it fosters a culture of research, entrepreneurship, and community engagement. Led by a dedicated faculty comprising seasoned academicians and industry experts, Pes Modern College of Engineering is a hub of intellectual growth and technological advancement, empowering its students to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of engineering and beyond.

Vision

"To create a collaborative academic environment to foster professional excellence and ethical values"

Mission

To develop outstanding professionals with high ethical standards capable of creating and managing global enterprises

To foster innovation and research by providing a stimulating learning environment

To foster and maintain mutually beneficial partnerships with alumni and industry
